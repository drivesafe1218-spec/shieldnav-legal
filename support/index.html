How can we help?

📧 Contact Support

For any questions, issues, or feedback, email us directly:

support@shieldnav.com

We aim to respond within 2 business days. Please include your device type, iOS version, and a description of the issue.

💳 Subscription & Billing

ShieldNav Premium subscriptions are managed entirely through the Apple App Store. For subscription and billing issues:

  • Cancel or manage your subscription: Go to Settings → [Your Apple ID] → Subscriptions on your iPhone.
  • Request a refund: Visit reportaproblem.apple.com — Apple handles all refund decisions.
  • Restore a past purchase: Open ShieldNav → Subscription page → tap "Restore" in the top right corner.

ShieldNav cannot issue refunds directly. All billing is processed by Apple.

🗺️ Crime Data & Map Issues

Crime data is sourced from public municipal databases and may be delayed or inaccurate. If you notice a data issue:

  • Use the in-app feedback tool: Settings → Development Feedback → Data Issue
  • Or email support@shieldnav.com with the city, location, and description of the issue.

See our Crime Data Disclaimer for information about data accuracy and limitations.

🔑 Account Issues

If you're having trouble signing in or accessing your account:

  • Forgot password: Use the "Forgot Password?" link on the sign-in screen to receive a reset email.
  • Email not verified: Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a verification email from ShieldNav. You can request a new one from the sign-in screen.
  • Delete your account: To permanently delete your account and all associated data, go to Settings → Account → Delete Account in the app, or email support@shieldnav.com with the subject "Delete My Account." We will process your request within 30 days.

🔒 Privacy & Data

For questions about how we collect and use your data, or to submit a data rights request (access, correction, deletion):

  • Read our Privacy Policy
  • Email support@shieldnav.com with the subject "Privacy Request"

California residents may also request information about our data sharing practices under the CCPA. See our Privacy Policy for details.
